EV charger terms:

Tethered EV chargers are those with a cable attached to the EV charger. This format is a Type 2 AC connector suitable for new electric vehicles. The Sigen DC EV charger comes tethered with a CCS2 connector, also suitable for new electric vehicles.

Untethered EV chargers are those in which the cable connecting the charger to the vehicle is a separate component, sold separately. This EV charger type suits either: 

Solar inverter energy ecosystem alignment
Public areas and 
Older model electric vehicles that require a Type 1 AC connection
